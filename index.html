Somnath Jyotirlinga

Somnath is the first among the twelve Jyotirlinga shrines of Shiva, located in Gujarat.

Mallikarjuna Jyotirlinga

Mallikarjuna is located on the Shri Shaila Mountain, Andhra Pradesh.

Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga

Mahakaleshwar is situated in Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh.

Omkareshwar Jyotirlinga

Omkareshwar is located on an island in the Narmada River, Madhya Pradesh.

Kedarnath Jyotirlinga

Kedarnath is located in the Himalayas, Uttarakhand.

Bhimashankar Jyotirlinga

Bhimashankar is situated in Maharashtra.

Kashi Vishwanath Jyotirlinga

Kashi Vishwanath is located in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh.

Trimbakeshwar Jyotirlinga

Trimbakeshwar is situated near Nashik, Maharashtra.

Vaidyanath Jyotirlinga

Vaidyanath is located in Deoghar, Jharkhand.

Nageshwar Jyotirlinga

Nageshwar is situated near Dwarka, Gujarat.

Rameshwaram Jyotirlinga

Rameshwaram is located in Tamil Nadu.

Grishneshwar Jyotirlinga

Grishneshwar is located near Ellora, Maharashtra.
